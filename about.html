Health and Hygiene

Health is divided into 3 different types

  • physical health
  • mental health
  • social wellbeing

physical health

  • It is defined as the normal functioning of the body

mental health

  • Mental health is a state of well-being that enables people to cope with life's stresses and realize their potential.

social wellbeing

  • Social well-being is the sharing, developing, and sustaining of meaningful relationships with others.
